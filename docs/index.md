# Welcome to Digital Marketing Agent Documentation

> Intelligent automation system for lead generation and engagement in legal services

## ðŸŽ¯ What is Digital Marketing Agent?

The **Digital Marketing Agent** is an AI-powered automation system designed to streamline lead generation and initial engagement for legal services businesses, specifically focusing on real estate conveyancing and new client acquisition.

### Key Capabilities

- **Multi-Channel Lead Generation** - Capture leads from Digital Ads, PR, Business Development, and Organic Marketing
- **Intelligent Lead Qualification** - Automated scoring and routing to Sales Agent
- **Real-Time Analytics** - Performance metrics and ROI tracking
- **Seamless Integration** - Works with Sales Agent and New Business Agent
- **Data Quality Assurance** - Validation and enrichment before case creation
